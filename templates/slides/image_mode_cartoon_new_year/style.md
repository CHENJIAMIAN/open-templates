# PPT Design Style Guide — Spring Festival Pony Blind Box Theme

---

## Style Definition

**Style:** Cartoon New Year / Cute Pony / Blind Box Style

## Color Palette

- **bg:** Chinese Red — Solid color with subtle paper texture. The background must be relevant to the Page Title or Presentation Theme.
- **text:** Gold — High contrast against red background.
- **graphics:** Light Yellow, Peru Orange-Brown, Coral Red.
- **lines:** Soft, rounded, medium weight with slight gradient.

## Typography

Rounded Sans-serif, Bold, Centered.

---

## Page Layouts

### Cover
Strict Central Composition. The visual focus (Title) is perfectly centered on both X and Y axes. Leave a massive, clean negative space in the dead center. The cute 3D pony and festive elements must be arranged along the periphery or bottom edges to frame the center, ensuring the central title area is unobstructed.

### Analysis
Clean, high-contrast central area (white/cream paper texture) for text. Festive elements (firecrackers, coins) frame the edges.

### Process
Winding Firecracker or Red Silk Ribbon path acting as the timeline. Pony head peeks from the side.

---

## Design Rules

### Pony Character
A Chibi Style cute 3D Pony (Year of the Horse mascot) with Light Yellow body and Orange-Brown mane. Features short round ears, a smooth round forehead, and a fluffy tail. The style is strictly Blind Box or Soft Clay, resembling a baby horse.

### Ratio Control
For content pages (Analysis, Process, Data), strict 80/20 split: 80% functional content area (massive clean text zone), 20% decorative elements (Pony and festive items). The 3D Pony must be small and placed in the bottom corner or edge as a subtle mascot, NOT taking up the center stage.

### Visual Priority
Content > Decoration. The text panel MUST dominate the screen.

### Festive Atmosphere
Enrich the scene with Chinese New Year elements: Spring Couplets (Use decorative patterns or matching language text), Firecrackers, Fireworks, Dumplings, Lanterns, and Gold Ingots.

### Language Consistency
The text language in the image MUST strictly match the input text language. DO NOT translate English content to Chinese, and vice versa. If the input title is English, all body text MUST be English.

### Trendy Festive Frame
A Red Caution Tape or Washi Tape style border MUST be perfectly horizontal and vertical, forming a strict rectangular frame along the absolute edges of the image. NO diagonal or tilted angles. The tape MUST align perfectly with the X and Y axes (0° and 90°), creating a neat picture frame effect with zero margin from the screen edges. The tape should feature repeating text patterns: "SPRING FESTIVAL", "SPRING FESTIVAL 2026".

### Full-Bleed Composition
The Chinese Red background must extend to the very edges of the image (Full Bleed).

### Flat Lay Perspective
Render the scene as a strict Flat Lay or Frontal View with 0-degree camera angle. All text panels and objects must be aligned parallel to the screen plane, avoiding any 3D perspective distortion or box-like depth.

---

## ★ Text Panel Rules (CRITICAL — Anti-Container Rules)

### Panel Shape & Dimensionality
The text area is a **strictly 2D flat card panel** — a pure surface with **ZERO depth, ZERO thickness, ZERO interior cavity**. Think of it as a **premium paper sheet, a flat ceramic tile viewed from directly above, or a sticker/decal flush against the background**. It must NEVER be rendered as a 3D object with walls, volume, or interior space.

### Panel Visual Reference
The text panel should resemble ONE of the following (all strictly flat, top-down view):
- A **red envelope (红包) that has been sliced open and laid completely flat** on the table surface.
- A **horizontal hanging scroll (横幅) pinned flat** — a single sheet, not a rolled tube.
- A **flat golden ticket or invitation card** lying on the red background.
- A **decorative paper mat (纸垫)** or flat ceramic coaster seen from 90° above.

All references share one trait: they are **surfaces without volume**.

### Panel Shape
Rounded rectangle. Soft corners. The panel lies perfectly flush with the background plane, as if printed or glued onto the red surface.

### Panel Surface Texture
The panel surface has a subtle **paper-grain or matte ceramic finish** — this is a **visual texture applied to a flat surface only**, NOT a cue to generate a 3D ceramic object. The texture adds tactile richness to the 2D card, similar to how a watercolor paper has visible grain but remains flat.

### Panel Border
- Thin golden line border (1-2px visual equivalent), with optional soft warm glow.
- The border must feel like a **printed gold foil edge or a stamped frame line** — NOT a raised rim, NOT a metal edge, NOT a container wall.
- No heavy outlines. No dark borders. The edge should be soft, elegant, and seamlessly blended into the background.

### Panel Material Consistency
Use Paper Cutout or Soft Clay **surface texture** on the panel to ensure it feels like part of the festive scene. This means the material treatment is about **surface appearance only** (grain, color, subtle shadow) — it must NOT imply 3D volume or physical thickness.

---

## Clean Text Zone

Guide the viewer's eye to the center by using a large, prominent flat text panel. The panel MUST dominate the composition.

For Cover pages: NO text panel. The title text floats freely in negative space directly on the red background.

For Content pages: The white/cream/gold flat panel occupies the central area, providing high readability against the Chinese Red background.

---

## Lighting & Texture

Use Soft Studio Lighting to create gentle highlights and soft shadows on the 3D clay decorative elements (Pony, lanterns, ingots, etc.), enhancing their rounded forms. Apply translucent material effect to make decorative elements look like high-quality vinyl or matte ceramic.

**Important:** The flat text panel receives only a very subtle drop shadow (to slightly lift it from the background) — it must NOT receive highlights or shadows that imply thickness, walls, or a 3D container shape.

---

## Camera & Depth

Use a Telephoto Lens (85mm+) for a compressed, flat perspective. Avoid wide-angle distortion. Use shallow depth of field (Bokeh) ONLY for background elements, keeping the foreground text zone sharp and flat. Maintain 3D clay or blind box texture on all **decorative** elements only. Ensure all elements have soft, rounded edges and no sharp corners.

---

## Text Control

If decorative text is needed, prioritize using the Page Title. Secondary options: "AILY", "AILY CREATIVE PPT", "CREATIVE PPT". Alternatively, use decorative lines. Avoid random gibberish.

---

## Negative Constraints (MANDATORY)

### Absolutely Prohibited — 3D Container Shapes:
- DO NOT render any 3D box, tray, tin, bowl, tub, crate, or physical container
- DO NOT render food packaging, aluminum foil containers, bento boxes, takeout boxes, or baking molds
- DO NOT give the text panel any visible depth, walls, sidewalls, rim height, or interior cavity
- DO NOT render an open-lid or hinged-lid container of any kind
- DO NOT render the text area as an object you could "put things inside" — it must be a FLAT surface only
- The text panel must have ZERO height/thickness when viewed from the side

### Absolutely Prohibited — Visual Artifacts:
- DO NOT print color codes (e.g., #FF0000) on the image
- DO NOT display HEX values or RGB values as text
- DO NOT display technical terms like "Q-version", "SSS", "Ratio" as text
- Text must be meaningful content, not technical parameters

### Absolutely Prohibited — Other:
- No UI elements, no page numbers
- No random or gibberish text

- 16:9 aspect ratio, high resolution
- Professional PPT design
- Clean typography
- Pure design output only

- **Visuals:** Cute 3D Pony placed at the bottom or corners, acting as a frame. Festive elements (lanterns, gold ingots) floating around the edges. Red Caution Tape frame MUST be tightly adhered to the four edges of the screen, featuring repeating text: "SPRING FESTIVAL", "SPRING FESTIVAL 2026". Keep the center EMPTY for the title.
- **Strategy:** NO text panels. The title text floats freely in the negative space. The background is a clean, festive stage.
- **Anti-Container Reminder:** The center area is pure negative space — no box, no tray, no container of any kind.

### Example 2: Content Page (With Flat Panel)

- **Task:** 4 key points, title "Clinical Cases".
- **Layout:** [Analysis] — A large **flat 2D white/cream card panel** centered on the red background. The panel is a pure surface — like a sheet of premium paper laid flat — with a thin golden foil border. ZERO depth, ZERO walls.
- **Visuals:** Red Caution Tape frame MUST be tightly adhered to the four edges of the screen, featuring repeating text: "SPRING FESTIVAL", "AILY CREATIVE PPT", "2026".
- **Strategy:** Text inside the flat panel for contrast against Chinese Red bg. Light Yellow accents.
- **Anti-Container Reminder:** The white area is a FLAT card, NOT an open box or tray. It has no walls, no interior, no lid.

### Example 3: Content Page (With Flat Panel + Pony)

- **Task:** 4 key points → Heavy content load → Need clean flat panel for readability.
- **Layout:** [Analysis] Template → Central **flat white paper card** (2D surface, no depth) for readability on Red bg.
- **Visuals:** Add Chibi Style Pony (bottom-left, small) + Red Caution Tape frame tightly adhered to edges (Text: "SPRING FESTIVAL 2026", "AILY CREATIVE PPT", "2026") + Light Yellow accents.
- **Anti-Container Reminder:** The panel is a flat sheet of paper. If viewed from the side, it would be paper-thin. No box shape.
